Frequently Asked Questions

Here you'll find answers to the most common questions about our services. If you need further assistance, feel free to contact our support team.

General Questions

What is ATLPay?

We are a secure online payment gateway service provider that allows your business to accept payments from customers worldwide. We function as your payment gateway provider, simplifying the online payment process for both you and your customers.

What types of payments can I accept with ATLPay?

You can accept all major credit and debit cards, as well as online payments through our system. Additionally, our system facilitates online payment gateway transactions for a wider range of payment methods.

Is ATLPay secure?

Absolutely! ATLPay is PCI DSS compliant and utilises advanced fraud prevention technology to ensure the safety of your business and your customers' information. We prioritise security as a leading international payment gateway.

Do I need a merchant account to use ATLPay?

In most cases, yes. A merchant account allows you to receive funds from your payment processor. We can assist you in setting up a merchant account if needed, making ATLPay a comprehensive global payment merchant service.

Is there a monthly fee to use ATLPay?

Yes, we offer various plans with different pricing structures. We can help you find the plan that best suits your business needs, ensuring you get the most out of our online payment gateway service.

Getting Started

How do I sign up for ATLPay?

Signing up is quite easy! Simply visit our website and follow the registration process to become an online payment gateway service provider.

How long does it take to get started with ATLPay?

Our streamlined signup process allows you to be up and running quickly. In most cases, you can be accepting payments within a few days, making ATLPay a time-saving payment gateway provider.

Do I need any technical expertise to use ATLPay?

No prior technical knowledge is required. We integrate seamlessly with most existing platforms, and our team is always available to assist you, ensuring a smooth transition to using our online payment gateway service.

What if I need help setting up ATLPay?

Our friendly customer support team is here to help! We offer a variety of resources, including tutorials and live chat support, to guide you through using our international payment gateway.

Does ATLPay integrate with my shopping cart software?

ATLPay integrates with a wide range of popular shopping cart platforms. Check our website for a full list of compatible platforms.

Transactions & Fees

What are your transaction fees?

Our fees are competitive and transparent. You can find detailed information about our pricing plans on our website, allowing you to make informed decisions when choosing ATLPay as your payment gateway provider.
